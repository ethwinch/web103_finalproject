## Reflection

### 1. What went well during this unit?

This unit was more challenging than expected, and our group did not make as much progress as we had hoped. However, we were able to explore several project ideas, gain a better understanding of the requirements, and identify potential issues early in the process. This gave us a clearer sense of what we need to prioritize in the next unit.

### 2. What were some challenges your group faced in this unit?

One challenge our group faced was inconsistent participation. At least one member decided to leave the course, while some other members were not consistently responsive to group communication. This made it more difficult to coordinate tasks, make decisions, and complete the unit’s work efficiently. 

### 3. What additional support will you need in upcoming units as you continue to work on your final project?

Having a designated group leader may help keep the project organized and make progress more consistent. It may also be helpful to establish clearer expectations for communication and participation, since collaboration becomes difficult when group members are not regularly responding or contributing to discussions.
