## About

### Description and Purpose

Bad Idea Vault is a web application where users can record, organize, and evaluate unusual, risky, expensive, or questionable ideas before deciding whether to act on them.

Users can submit an idea with a title, description, category, estimated cost, risk level, and current status. Ideas can be reviewed, updated, rated, saved for later, or marked as approved, rejected, completed, or abandoned.

The purpose of Bad Idea Vault is to give users a fun and organized place to store ideas that they may not be ready to pursue. Instead of forgetting an idea or acting on it immediately, users can examine the possible risks, costs, and benefits first.


### Inspiration

Bad Idea Vault was inspired by the way people (like me) often have random ideas but do not have a good place to record or evaluate them.

Some ideas may be creative and useful, while others may be expensive, unrealistic, or risky. Notes apps can store these ideas, but they usually do not help users compare them, rate their risk, or track what eventually happened.

Bad Idea Vault turns idea tracking into a more interactive experience. The app uses a humorous theme while still helping users think carefully, organize their thoughts, and decide whether an idea is worth pursuing.
